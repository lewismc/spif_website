Applications & Proposals

Your primary resource for all application procedures.

Proposal Guidelines

  1. Presubmission Information
    • Goal : To enrich PostDoc (academic, life ...) experiences at Stanford.
    • Who can submit : Current PostDoc at Stanford.
    • When : Anytime
    • Budget (2012-2013) : To be determined.
    • Submission overview : A preliminary letter of intent of 200-word, if approved, followed by a full proposal.
    • Example proposals : Multi-disciplinary journal clubs, interest group social gatherings, seminar series, professional development workshosp, job fairs, and so on.
    • Ineligible proposals : Research projects, travel expenses, and alcohol expenses within a proposal, groups directly affiliated with other organizations.
  2. Application Procedure
  3. Proposal Review
    • Period : Proposal reviewed at the third weekend of each month and decisions sent to applicants at the end of the fourth week.
    • Criteria :
      1. Does the proposal fill a need/gap in the PostDoc experience at Stanford?
      2. Does the proposal attract a general PostDoc group?
      3. Does the proposal fit the budget of SPIF?
  4. Reimbursement
    • Please follow these guidelines here : spif-refund-procedure.pdf and fill in the Reimbursement Detail Form . Note : All the required documents, detailed in the spif-refund-procedure.pdf, shall be combined into a single PDF and email to the treasurer within 5 days of the event to ensure the 10-day time limit is met or else you may forfeit your reimbursement.
  5. Proposal Follow-up / Progress Report
    • Required when submitting a renewal proposal. Please complete the Follow-up Form.
  6. Renew Proposals
    • Applicants should submit the "Progress Report" and go through the "Full Proposal Submission", indicating that its a renewal proposal.

Application Procedure

New Proposals

Not quite sure whether your proposal fits? Submit a Letter of Intent first! A Letter of Intent contains a 200-word description of your project, including contact name, email and affiliation, brief project description, targeted postdocs, to spif.supd@gmail.com. The SPIF committee will review your idea before going to the full application.

You can also skip the above process and directly go Full Proposal Submission.

Renewal Proposals

Please submit a Progress Report/Follow-up Form and go through the Full Proposal Submission, indicating its a renewal proposal in the "Additional Information / Comments" section.
