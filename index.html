Since 2010, SPIF has successfully funded diverse projects including multi-disciplinary journal clubs, seminar series, social gatherings (movie, BBQ ...), professional development workshops and so on. For a list of funded projects, refer to the end of this page.

We Foster PostDoc Initiatives

The primary objective of the Stanford PostDoc Initiatives Fund (SPIF) is to recognize up and coming initiaives from within the Stanford PostDoc community. We have a proven record of backing projects from inception onwards and watching them grow.

Part of SUPD

The Stanford University Postdoctoral Association, SUPD, is a volunteer organization formed by Stanford University PostDocs as an advocacy forum with the aim of enhancing and enriching the PostDoc experience at Stanford. We join them in this effort.
